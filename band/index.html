BIO::ORIGIN

Heavy Metal. Metalcore. Post Grunge. Why not mix them all?

Born in the ABC region of São Paulo, Brazil, Insane Driver was forged from raw ideas and an uncompromising desire to create a sound that defies the ordinary.

With creative arrangements, crushing breakdowns and lyrics that confront the everyday — reflections on society, inner battles and the unapologetic energy of Rock N' Roll — the quintet delivers a wall of sound that is equally heavy, harmonic and dynamic.

"You need to be Insane to keep your sanity."
