Connect with Insane Driver on Spotify

It recently came to us that that people were afraid to pre-save our songs, trusting their data to third-party services.

We agree that everyone should care about privacy, thus we decided to create our own pre-save service. We guarantee that we won't keep any private information, as we don't intend to provide you with any advertisements or other services whatsoever - we only care to deliver you our music in the best possible way.

Here's what this service really does:

  • Follows Insane Driver's profile
  • Follows Silicon Fortress's singles playlist
  • Pre-saves Insane Driver's songs
  • You can optionally add them to a playlist of your choosing
